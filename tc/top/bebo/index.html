    Official logo for Bands!

    Now big name artists and groups signed to the majors will all have an "official" logo! Don't see the logo on the profile you're a fan of? Now you can search to see if they have an official profile! The official one will appear at the top of the search, you can't miss it.

    This does not mean the end of fan profiles, as we think it's important for fans to connect with other fans, but now you'll be able to connect with the artist as well!
